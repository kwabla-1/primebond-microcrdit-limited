Who we are

PrimeBond Micro-Credit Enterprise is non deposit taking financial firm that specializes in lending short term emergency loans to individuals, small and medium scale businesses, Vehicle owners and Churches.

Vision statement

PrimeBond Micro-Credit Enterprise is to be the leading and preferred micro-credit institution offering premium monetary assistance for achieving widespread financial growth for SMEs

Mission statement

PrimeBond Micro-Credit Enterprise's mission is to provide holistic support to business through quick and easily accessible micro-credit services with a strong commitment to positive socio-economic change

Core values

The company’s core values for clients and stakeholders are; Loyalty, Hard work, Discipline and Commitment.
